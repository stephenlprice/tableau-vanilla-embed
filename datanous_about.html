What is Datanous?

or: how I learned to stop worrying and ignore the man in the labcoat

Nous or noēsis: knowledge of what is true and what is false. Real and unreal. Good and bad.
It is not reason, it is not perception and it is not imagination.
It enables these faculties working within the mind's eye.

Anaxagoras claimed:

For it is the thinnest of all things and the purest, and it has all knowledge about everything and the greatest strength; and nous has power over all things, both greater and smaller, that have soul [psychē].

Data-noēsis: access, visualization, transformation, description, modeling, sharing - understanding.
When most dianoia is run by machines what will your humanity be made of?
